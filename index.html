About Us

Trusted by industries across Louisiana

Louisiana Structured Cabling (LASC) provides fiber optic and category cabling services for businesses and industries across Louisiana. Founder and president of the company, Matthew Theriot, was born and raised in Lake Charles, Louisiana, where the LASC is based. He has had over 30 years of experience designing and installing custom cabling solutions for industries throughout the US. Now he brings that expertise to businesses in Louisiana.
